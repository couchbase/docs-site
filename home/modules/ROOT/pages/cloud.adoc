= Welcome to Couchbase Capella

Capella operational is the easiest way to use Couchbase Server.
Get access to SQL-like querying, Full-Text Search (FTS), powerful eventing, and connectivity to mobile and IoT devices at the edge.

Capella columnar is an analytical database (RT-OLAP) for real-time apps and operational intelligence.
Capella columnar is a standalone, cloud-only offering from Couchbase under the Capella family of products.

Capella AI services is a fully managed set of tools that help you build, deploy, and scale your agentic and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) applications.
